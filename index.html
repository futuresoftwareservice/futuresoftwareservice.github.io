ABOUT US

Established by collaborating IT professionals from various major European IT companies, the company is a specialized software distributor providing services to the IT sector. In 2017, the company established Future Software Distribution s.r.o., a subsidiary in the Czech Republic for primarily distributing software in Eastern Europe and outsourcing IT services.

MAIN ACTIVITIES

The company distributes the FUTURE DEVELOPMENT KIT™ (FDK), a special and unique development tool for optimizing the design and development of software solutions. The tool consists of a set of content management libraries, features and repositories designed for highly effective and reliable application development on the Microsoft Visual Studio platform.
We also offer other unique products for development:

FUTURE MOBILE DEVELOPMENT KIT™

This is a software tool for developing mobile applications on the JAVA platform and Android operating system.

FUTURE TRAFFIC BIGDATA KIT™

This is a software platform for processing BIG DATA in the transportation sector. The tool optimizes the use of structured and non-structured transportation data from vehicle movements through toll gates, modules for monitoring vehicle movement, or SIM card movements for mobile operators.
